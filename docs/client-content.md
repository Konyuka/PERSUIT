# Persuit Consultants - Services and Target Audience

## Overview

Persuit Consultants is a specialized consulting firm focused on the **East African hospitality and travel technology sector**. The company operates as a bridge between international technology providers and local hospitality businesses (hotels, travel agents, tour operators).

---

## Service Categories

### 1. Tech for Hotels Partnerships / Tech for Travel/Tours Partnerships

**Target Audience:** Hotels, Airbnb, Apartments, etc.

#### Features:

**1. Market Entry & Business Development Support**

*For International Tech Companies:*
- Identify and qualify potential travel agent partners in East Africa
- Local market research on trends, demand, and competitor landscape
- Introductions to vetted agents with verified business capabilities

*For Travel Agents/Hotels:*
- Access to international technology solutions (booking platforms, CRM, payment systems)
- Guidance on evaluating solutions and ROI

**2. Partnership Facilitation & Deal Structuring**
- Introduce and negotiate deals between tech companies and travel agents/Hotels
- Draft basic MoUs or partnership frameworks
- Facilitate pilot programs or trial runs for software or services

**3. Technology Adoption & Integration Support**

*For Travel Agents/Hotels:*
- Assistance with implementing booking platforms, CRM systems, payment gateways, and revenue management tools
- Training and onboarding support
- Customization guidance for local needs (currency, language, payment methods)

**4. Marketing & Go-to-Market Support**

*For Tech Companies:*
- Introduce their solutions to target travel agents through workshops, webinars, and demos
- Co-branded marketing campaigns targeting local travel agents/Hotels
- Feedback collection to refine product-market fit

*For Travel Agents:*
- Training on leveraging tech tools for marketing, upselling, and digital presence

**5. Training & Capacity Building**
- Workshops on digital marketing, online bookings, customer management, and revenue optimization
- Continuous support and best practice sharing
- Technical support for troubleshooting adoption issues

**6. Networking & Events**
- Organize virtual or physical networking events, meetups, and conferences
- Connect international tech companies with clusters of travel agents
- Facilitate B2B matchmaking sessions

**7. Ongoing Account Management & Support**
- Regular check-ins with both tech companies and travel agents/Hotels
- Monitor adoption progress, usage rates, and satisfaction
- Suggest improvements or additional solutions

**8. Revenue & Performance Analytics (Optional)**
- Track ROI for tech companies entering the East African market
- Provide insights on adoption, bookings, and growth opportunities
- Recommend performance improvements

#### Problems Solved:
1. Speeds up partnerships and ensures quality connections
2. Reduced friction in cross-border partnerships and builds trust
3. Travel agents/Hotels successfully adopt international tech tools without operational disruption
4. Ensuring adoption and maximizes the value of technology investments
5. Travel agents/hotels becomes self-sufficient and confident using new tech solutions
6. Builds trust and long-term business relationships in East Africa
7. Reduces churn, improves retention, and enhances long-term partnerships
8. Demonstrates measurable value to both sides

**8. Customer Segmentation & Targeted Offers**

**10. Training & Capacity Building**
